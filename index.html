Who We Are

Welcome to Cornell University's first quantitative finance organization. Our mission is to establish a community of financial innovators to explore the bleeding-edge of investing technology.

Our Three Arms

Education

Analysts will hone their quantitative knowledge and skills in our rigorous training cirriculum. Topics include financial theory, probability, statistics, game theory, & more.

Research

Research is at the core of developing innovative ideas for investing. Members will embark on deep dive research projects to develop original alpha generating strategies.

Investing

Only the best strategies will be tested in real-time with our cross-asset portfolio. Investing team members will also be eligible to represent us in national competitions.
